ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE FOR EVERYONE

    WHAT IS ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE?

  • The term Artificial Intelligence is composed of two words i.e. Artificial & Intelligence. The word Artificial means non-natural and Intelligence refers to the ability to understand, think and learn.

  • The AI can be defined as a man-made thinking power.

    •

    GOALS OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

  • Creating expert systems.

  • Implementing Intelligence in machines.

    •

    TYPES OF ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

  • NARROW AI - Artificial intelligence is said to be narrow when the machine can perform a specific (single) task extremely well, even better than humans.

  • GENERAL AI - Artificial intelligence reaches teh general state when it can perform any intellectual task with the same accuracy level as a human would.

  • STRONG AI - Strong AI is a level of Intelligence of Systems in which machines could surpass human intelligence and can perform any task better than human with cognitive properties.
