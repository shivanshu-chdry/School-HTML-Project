MS ACCESS: FORMS, QUERIES & REPORTS

    ABOUT FORMS

  • Forms are the most flexible ways to view, edit and delete data.

  • MS-Access forms have some major advantages over the traditional paper forms as they save your time, effort and paper.

  • SPLIT FORM - A Split Form is a form in which the same data is displayed in two views simultaneously. One part of the form will display a Form view, while the other part displays a Datesheet view.

  • MULTIPLE ITEMS FORM - Multiple items form displays multiple records in a single window, one record per row instead of regular form where one record is displayed in a single window.

    •

    FEATURES OF ACCESS FORMS

  • It is a window or screen that contains numerous fields or spaces to enter data.

  • They are either based on table or query.

  • Form created from a table will let you view the records that are already in that table and you can also add new records.

    •

    UNDERSTANDING QUERY

  • Queries allow you to pull information from one or more tables based on a set of search conditions you define.

  • Running a query is like asking a detailed question based on your database.

  • Queries are far more powerful than the simple searches or filters you might use to find data within a table.
