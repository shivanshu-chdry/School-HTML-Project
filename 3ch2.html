    DEEP LEARNING

  • Deep learning can be considered as a subset of machine learning. It is a field that is based on learning and improving on its own by examining computer algorithms.

  • Deep learning is a key technology behind driver-less cars, enabling them to recognize a stop sign, or to distinguish a pedestrian from a lamppost.

    DATA SCIENCE

  • The simplest definition of data science is the extraction of actionable insights from raw data.

  • Data Science helps you manage data, analyze it and create meaning for the data that we have.

  • All this is possible by applying data analytics to the data or information and then coming up with some meaning that is understandable by the end user.

    NEURAL NETWORKS

  • Neural Networks are inspired by human brains and copies the working process of human brains.

  • A neural network is a network or circuit of neurons or in a modern sense, an artificial neural network, commposed of artificial neurons or nodes.
