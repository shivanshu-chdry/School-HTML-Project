    UNDERSTANDING NETWORK SECURITY

  • There are many people who attempt to damage our Internet-connected computers, violate our privacy and make it impossible to use the Internet services.

  • Given the frequency and variety of existing attacks as well as the threat of new and more destructive future attacks, network security has become a central topic in the field of cyber security.

  • Network security in its simplest term, is a set of rules and configurations designed to protect the computer networks and data using both software and hardware technologies.
